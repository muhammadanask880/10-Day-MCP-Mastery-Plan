# 📝 Day 1 Quiz — 5 Questions

Answer all 5 in your own words. Don't look back at the lessons until you've tried.

When done, use the **self-evaluate** trick from this folder's `README.md` to grade yourself with any AI.

---

### Question 1
Fill in the blank:

> *"MCP solves the **____ × ____** problem by turning it into **____ + ____**."*

---

### Question 2
What do the three letters in MCP stand for, **and what does each one mean in plain English?**

---

### Question 3
Finish this sentence so you could say it to anyone:

> *"MCP is like USB-C for ____________________."*

---

### Question 4 — Scenario
Your client says: *"I want to use Gemini instead of Claude for our internal assistant."*

You have a Slack automation built on MCP. **How much code do you have to rewrite, and why?**

---

### Question 5 — True or False
*"MCP is owned by Anthropic, so only Claude can use it."*

True or False? **Explain your answer in one line.**
